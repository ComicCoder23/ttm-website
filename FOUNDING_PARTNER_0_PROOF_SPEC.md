# Gill — Founder Partner 0 / Free Proof Pilot Spec

Internal reference document for the Gill pilot engagement.
Gill is **Founder Partner 0** — a free proof pilot, not a paying client. This engagement exists to establish proof of concept, generate a first portfolio case, and validate TTM's workflow before charging for it.

---

## Engagement Summary

| Field | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Founder Slot** | 0 (proof pilot — outside the 5 paid founder slots) |
| **Engagement Type** | Free — no charge to Gill |
| **Reason for Free Engagement** | Proof of concept, portfolio foundation, workflow validation |
| **Primary TTM Benefit** | First real case study, before/after evidence, testimonial, referral opportunity |

---

## What Gill Receives

- Full TTM service delivery (package to be confirmed with Gill)
- No invoice, no payment required
- Same quality and attention as a paying founding partner

---

## What TTM Asks in Return

All of the following are requested but treated as genuinely optional — this is a relationship, not a contract:

| Ask | Detail |
|---|---|
| **Testimonial** | Honest written testimonial via WhatsApp or email in Gill's own words — only if genuinely happy with the result |
| **Portfolio Rights** | Permission to use before/after screenshots of Gill's online presence in TTM's portfolio and marketing materials |
| **Measurable Uplift Notes** | Gill to share any observable changes (more calls, enquiries, Google visibility) at the 30-day mark, even if anecdotal |
| **Workflow Timing Notes** | TTM to document time taken at each stage to establish delivery benchmarks for future clients |
| **Referral Intro** | If Gill is happy, one intro to another trade business who could benefit — no pressure, entirely optional |
